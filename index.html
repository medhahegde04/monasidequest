About me

Computers always felt natural to me — easy in the way things feel easy when they just make sense. Math worked the same way: just knowing what to appply where, when, and how. I think that's the thread running through most of what I do. Finding the logic in something, then finding the fun in it. Right now that means a lot of things at once — data science, game development, and building this website.

I'm not sure where it all leads yet, and I'm okay with that. That same curiosity doesn't stay inside a screen either:

  • I like art. Experimenting with new mediums, new methods, trying to figure out what works, and the process of creating something is fun.
  • I like trying out new games. Understanding the game mechanics, graphics, storyline, and figuring out what makes a game interesting—even the ones that I personally don't like is intriguing.
  • I'm even trying to get into gardening. Learning the different needs of different plants, and caring for them is a slow and therapeutic process, reminding me to take things slow sometimes.

All of this keeps reminding me to take it one day at a time and to trust the process.
